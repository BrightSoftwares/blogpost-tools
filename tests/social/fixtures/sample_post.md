## The Problem

Managing Gmail with dozens of rules is a nightmare. Every new sender needs a new rule.

## The Solution

Rule Chains split matching from action. One front rule per category, one action rule per treatment.

## Results

12 rules replace 46. Change one category setting and every sender in it updates at once.
